-Book Now, Get a free quote, Contact Us
-On the website, pricing should be immediately visible along with before and after pictures
-The site will have images and videos because this will be important to draw those upper class customers.

Part 2: Design
-Modern and Minimalist Design
-Black, White, Silver, Dark Blue Accents color scheme
-Headings: Montserrat 
Body text: Open Sans
-Proper spacing will be used between sections, text blocks, and images to avoid clutter. Clear margins and padding will make the content easier to read and visually balanced.
-Large headings for section titles, bold descriptions, clear images, clear call-to-action buttons will be the visual hierarchy.
-Icons will represent services like car wash, interior cleaning, ceramic coating, contact information
-The design should invoke trust, professionalism, luxury, and quality
-Accessibility considerations will include high color contrast readability, large and readable font sizes, simple navigation, and mobile-friendly design
-This design is inspired by modern automotive websites and luxury brand websites that emphasize large visuals, simple layouts, and minimal text 
-The layout of the homepage will be the navigation bar, hero section with image and call-to-action, services overview, gallery preview, about section, contact section, footer

Part 3:
-The website will include, navigation menu, buttons, contact form, image gallery
-Users will navigate using a top navigation bar that links to each page. The menu will remain consistent across all pages.
-The contact form will include, name is entered, email is valid, message field is not empty. If the form is incomplete, users will receive an error message.
-JavaScript will be used for form validation, interactive gallery display
-Interactivity improves the user experience by making the website more engaging and easier to use. It also helps guide users toward completing actions such as contacting the business or booking a service.
