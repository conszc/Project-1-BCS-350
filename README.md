# Project-1-BCS-350
Part 1: Content

What is the name of your business?
The name of the business is Precision Mobile Detailing.

Is the business real or fictional?
The business is fictional.

What products or services does the business offer?
Precision Mobile Detailing offers professional car detailing services including interior deep cleaning, headlight restoration, paint correction, and ceramic coating.

Who is the target audience?
The target audience consists primarily of middle- to upper-class professionals, such as doctors, lawyers, and CEOs, who value high-quality vehicle care and convenience.

What problem does this business solve?
The business provides convenient, professional vehicle maintenance services that help customers protect the appearance, value, and longevity of their vehicles without needing to visit a physical location.

What makes this business unique compared to competitors?
Precision Mobile Detailing stands out by offering high-end detailing services directly at the customer’s location, focusing on convenience, premium-quality products, and meticulous attention to detail for luxury and professional clientele.

What pages will your website include (e.g., Home, About, Services, Contact)?
The website will include the following pages: Home, About, Services, Contact, Booking, Gallery, and Testimonials.

Write a short mission statement for the business.
At Precision Mobile Detailing, our mission is to provide exceptional and professional car detailing services that enhance and preserve the beauty of every vehicle. We are committed to using the highest-quality products, meticulous techniques, and personalized care to deliver an unmatched experience for our clients and their prized automobiles.

What tone should the website content have (professional, friendly, modern, etc.)?
The website should have a friendly yet professional tone to create a welcoming experience while still reflecting the premium nature of the services.

What call-to-action do you want visitors to take?
Visitors should be encouraged to Book Now, Get a Free Quote, or Contact Us.

What information must be immediately visible on the homepage?
The homepage should clearly display service pricing and before-and-after photos so visitors can quickly understand the value and quality of the detailing services.

Will the site include images, videos, or other media? Why?
Yes, the website will include images and videos. Visual media is important for showcasing the quality of the detailing work, demonstrating results, and appealing to the expectations of upper-class customers who value high standards and presentation.

Part 2: Design

What overall style will the website have (minimalist, corporate, creative, etc.)?
The website will have a modern and minimalist design. This style keeps the layout clean and professional while allowing the vehicle images and services to stand out.

What color scheme will you use and why?
The website will use a black, white, and silver color scheme with dark blue accents. These colors help create a luxurious and professional appearance, which aligns with the premium nature of the detailing services and appeals to higher-end customers.

What fonts will you use for headings and body text?
The website will use Montserrat for headings and Open Sans for body text. Montserrat provides bold, modern titles, while Open Sans is clean and easy to read for longer paragraphs.

How will spacing and layout improve readability?
Proper spacing will be used between sections, text blocks, and images to avoid clutter. Clear margins and padding will make the content easier to read and create a balanced, organized layout.

What visual hierarchy will guide the user’s attention?
The visual hierarchy will include large section headings, bold service descriptions, high-quality images, and clear call-to-action buttons. This structure helps guide the user’s attention from important information to actions such as booking a service.

How will consistency be maintained across pages?
Consistency will be maintained by using the same color palette, fonts, spacing, and navigation layout across all pages. Buttons, headings, and service sections will follow a consistent style so users have a smooth and predictable browsing experience.

What icons will you use?
Icons will be used to represent different services and information, such as car wash services, interior cleaning, ceramic coating, and contact information.

What emotions should the design evoke in users?
The design should evoke trust, professionalism, luxury, and quality, helping customers feel confident about choosing the service.

How will accessibility be considered (contrast, font size, etc.)?
Accessibility will be addressed by using high color contrast for readability, large and clear font sizes, simple navigation, and a mobile-friendly responsive design so the website is easy to use on all devices.

What inspiration sources influenced your design?
The design is inspired by modern automotive and luxury brand websites that emphasize large visuals, simple layouts, and minimal text to create a premium user experience.

Part 3:

What interactive elements will the website include?
The website will include several interactive elements such as a navigation menu, clickable buttons, a contact form, and an image gallery. These features allow users to easily explore the website and interact with its content.

How will users navigate through the site?
Users will navigate the website using a top navigation bar that links to each page, including Home, About, Services, Gallery, Booking, and Contact. The navigation menu will remain consistent across all pages to ensure a smooth and predictable browsing experience.

What forms will the site include and what fields will they have?
The website will include a contact form where users can enter their name, email address, and a message to request information or ask questions about services.

How will form validation work?
Form validation will ensure that the name field is filled out, the email address is entered in a valid format, and the message field is not left empty. If any of these fields are incomplete or incorrect, the user will receive an error message prompting them to correct the issue before submitting the form.

What role will JavaScript play in the website?
JavaScript will be used to provide form validation and an interactive image gallery. This allows the website to check user input in real time and create a more dynamic browsing experience when viewing images.

How does interactivity improve the user experience?
Interactivity improves the user experience by making the website more engaging and easier to use. It also helps guide users toward completing important actions, such as contacting the business or booking a detailing service, which supports the overall goals of the website.
